Nimcet 2020 App and website is developed by Rajat kathuriya. He has been selected in Nimcet exam in 2018 and currently studying MCA in Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology Allahabad.

This App and website is developed to help the students regarding the exam.
The NIMCET is a Common Entrance National Level Test, conducted by any of the NITs ,for admission in to their MCA (Master of Computer Applications ) programme. The admissions are given on merit basis to Master of Computer Applications (MCA) programme across the NITs located in Agartala, Allahabad, Kurukshetra, Calicut, Raipur, Warangal, Surathkal,Trichy, Jamshedpur, Bhopal and Kurukshetra.

This app is for mca aspirants 2020 who are preparing for MCA entrance exams. This app is made for the students who are targeting nimcet exam in 2020.
App contains following features -
1. Syllabus
2. Practice questions.
3. Coaching centers
4. Recommended books.
5. Placement Records.
6. Eligibility and Rank criteria.
7. All other exam updates like -

BHU (Banaras Hindu University)
AMU (Aligarh Muslim University)

HCU (Hyderabad central University)
MAH CET (Maharastra Common entrance test)
UPSEE
